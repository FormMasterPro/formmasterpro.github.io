Cancellation Policy

We believe you should be free to unsubscribe anytime, without hassle

Our Hassle-Free Approach

At FormMasterPro, we believe that you should have complete control over your subscription. That's why we've made cancelling as simple and straightforward as possible. No phone calls required, no exit surveys to complete, and no attempts to convince you to stay.

Our Promise to You:

  • Cancel anytime - You can unsubscribe whenever you want
  • No questions asked - No justification needed
  • Self-service cancellation - No need to contact support
  • No hidden fees - Zero cancellation penalties
  • Clear communication - We'll confirm your cancellation immediately

How to Cancel Your Subscription

Step 1: Log In

Sign in to your FormMasterPro account at app.formmasterpro.com

Step 2: Account Settings

Click on your profile icon in the top-right corner and select "Account Settings" from the dropdown menu.

Step 3: Subscription Tab

Navigate to the "Subscription" tab in your account settings.

Step 4: Cancel Subscription

Click the "Cancel Subscription" button at the bottom of the page.

Step 5: Confirmation

Confirm your cancellation. You'll receive an email confirmation immediately.

That's it! Your subscription will be cancelled immediately, though you'll maintain access to premium features until the end of your current billing period.

Frequently Asked Questions About Cancellation

Will I get a refund when I cancel?

For monthly subscriptions, we don't provide partial refunds for the current billing month, but you won't be charged again. For annual subscriptions, you may be eligible for a prorated refund of unused months.

Additionally, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for new subscribers. If you cancel within 30 days of your initial subscription, you can request a full refund by contacting support.

What happens to my data when I cancel?

Your saved data, form mappings, and account information will remain intact even after you cancel. You'll simply lose access to premium features and revert to the free tier functionality.

Can I rejoin after cancelling?

Absolutely! You can resubscribe at any time. Simply log in to your account and visit the subscription page to sign up again.

What if I need help cancelling?

While our cancellation process is designed to be self-service, we're happy to assist if you encounter any difficulties. Simply email us at support@formmasterpro.com and we'll help you cancel your subscription promptly.
